Lime3DS

About

Lime3DS is an open-source, easy-to-use Nintendo 3DS emulator, forked from Citra. Lime has many features, such as firmware emulation, amiibo loading, and partial support for homebrew applications.

FAQ

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions we get from the Lime3DS Discord server.

"Why won't Lime launch on my computer?"
There are two executables for Lime3DS, 'lime.exe' and 'lime-qt.exe'. Make sure you are launching 'lime-qt.exe'.

"Why does Lime keep crashing?"
One of the most common reasons for Lime crashing is insufficient and/or unsupported hardware. 4GB+ of RAM is recommended in order to properly run Lime3DS.

What are the minimum specs to run Lime on my device?"
PC:
Operating System: Windows 10/11 (64-bit), MacOS Big Sur, or modern 64-bit Linux.
CPU: x68-64 (64-bit) CPU. Single core performance higher than 1,800 on Passmark.
GPU: OpenGL 4.3 or Vulkan 1.1 support.
Ram: 2GB of free RAM. 4GB is recommended.

Android:
Operating System: Android 9.0+.
CPU: AArch64. Snapdragon 835 SoC or better.
GPU: OpenGL ES 3.2 or Vulkan 1.1 support.


Have any questions? Please refer to the GitHub repository or the Discord server.
